## Free Hosting Reality Check

### Why Free Hosting is Not Viable for mhma.us:
- **Limited resources**: Very slow, can't handle traffic
- **Forced ads**: Free hosts display ads on your site
- **No custom domain**: Usually gives you a subdomain (yourname.freehost.com)
- **Poor security**: Vulnerable to hacking
- **No support**: No customer service
- **Unreliable**: Frequent downtime
- **Limited features**: No plugins, limited storage, no backups
- **Bad for SEO**: Search engines rank free hosting poorly

### Free Hosting Options (Not Recommended):
- **WordPress.com free**: Limited, ads, no custom domain
- **000webhost**: Very limited, slow, ads
- **InfinityFree**: Unreliable, poor performance
- **GitHub Pages**: Static only, not for WordPress
