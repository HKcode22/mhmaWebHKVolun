OUR RESPONSIBILITY

  • Allah (SWT) has showered upon us an abundance of bounties. From among all the bounties that Allah (SWT) has favored us with, without a doubt the greatest bounty is being apart of this great and beautiful religion, Islam. It is an obligation upon us to learn about Islam and impart it to the next generation. If we do not perform this duty we will be held accountable in the court of Allah (SWT) on the Day of Judgment.
  • Our Prophet mentioned, “All of you are shepherds and you will all be asked about your flock.” (Bukhari) This narration teaches us two things. First, every person is responsible of someone or something else. Second, everyone will be questioned by Allah (SWT) as to how we carried out this responsibility.
  • Our children are our greatest responsibility. This is such a responsibility that the entire community will be held accountable and every parent as well. It is an obligation upon us, as a community member and as a parent, to impart this religion to our children.

GOAL

The goal of this Maktab program is to provide children with the opportunity to learn proper Qur’anic recitation, obtain a comprehensive Islamic education, and receive the necessary training to become familiar with Islamic norms and values (Tarbiayyah), to remain steadfast through the challenges they face in society

REGISTRATION

Please register your child. After registration we will call your child for placement if the spot is available. Otherwise, we will place your child on the waitlist.

What are the Makhtab program rules?Asif Mohammed2026-03-02T15:51:07-08:00

What are the Makhtab program rules?

  1. Students must arrive on time and be picked up on time every day.
  2. Students must come to class wearing clean clothes.
  3. Students must come to class wearing modest attire (loose clothing).  Full hijab must be Observed by the girls and boys are encouraged to wear Kufi (headgear). Students are not allowed to sit in class wearing shorts, tight or indecent clothing.
  4. Students must be respectful to the teachers, peers, and the facility at all times. Any form of disrespect will NOT be tolerated at all.
  5. Students are not allowed to leave the facility premises without permission from the teacher or staff member on duty.
  6. Students are not allowed to use any electronic devices in the class.
  7. Students are not allowed to bring any item to class that can be a distraction to themselves or other students of the class.
  8. Students are not allowed to bring food or drinks to the class.
  9. Students are not allowed to bring any dangerous item to class, knife etc.
  10. Students must be punctual with their attendance in our school.
  11. Students must complete their homework on time.
  12. Parents must sign the child’s progress report every week. If it is not signed, the teacher will send a reminder to the parents. If the problem continues then it could impact your child’s participation in our program.

Violations:

  • The first violation will result in a talk with the student.
  • The second violation will result in a meeting with the parents.
  • Third violation will result in suspension or possible expulsion. The consequences are at the discretion of the teachers and staff.
What are the guidelines for the parents?Asif Mohammed2024-06-01T19:41:00-07:00

What are the guidelines for the parents?

  • Parents should show importance to their child’s Islamic education, if we do not show importance how can we expect our children to show importance.
  • Like any other type of education, a necessary component of your child’s success starts from your own home.  The more effort you make in your child’s studies the more he will succeed.
  • Parents should be informed of what their child is learning in school.
  • Parents should ensure their child arrives to class on time and leaves class on time. Every minute matters!
  • They should check their WhatsApp daily for any updates by teachers & admins. This will be the main way of communication between staff and parents.
  • Parents are NOT allowed to have conversations with one another near the class where it becomes a distraction to the class.
What is the Curriculum of the Maktab program?Asif Mohammed2026-03-02T15:56:36-08:00

What is the Curriculum of the Maktab program?

  1. Quran – teaching Qaidah with correct pronunciation, reading Quran, and memorizing of surahs.
  2. Tajweed – Teaching tajweed rules with its application.
  3. Islamic studies – Consisting of basic Aqeedah, Seerah, fiqh, quranic stories, hadiths and other general Islamic Studies.
    • Fiqh: Learning the laws regarding wudhu, ghusl, salaah, and other daily ibadaat
    • Surahs & Du’aas: Memorizing short surahs and du’aas with their translation and understanding.
